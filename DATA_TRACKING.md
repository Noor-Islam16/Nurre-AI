Nuree AI — What We Track

All data is stored on our own infrastructure. No third-party analytics. All tracking requires user consent.


User Behavior
- Page views and navigation patterns
- Idle time and tab switching
- Frustration signals (rapid clicking, text deletion)
- Scroll behavior

Tasks
- Task creation, updates, completion, and deletion
- Priority, due dates, and recurring patterns
- Average completion time

Focus Sessions
- Start, pause, resume, and end times
- Planned vs actual duration
- Completion rate and pause reasons
- Breathing exercise sessions

Mood & Energy
- Self-reported mood (terrible / bad / okay / good / excellent)
- Energy and focus levels (1–10)
- AI-detected mood with confidence score
- Mood trends over time

AI Interactions
- Chat messages (user and assistant)
- AI tool usage (creating tasks, setting timers, etc.)
- Intervention triggers, timing, and whether they were accepted or dismissed

Productivity Patterns
- Most productive hours
- Common procrastination triggers
- Preferred session length
- Successful work patterns
- Stress, motivation, and overwhelm indicators

Clinical Assessments
- Assessment type, completion, and severity
- Individual responses

Music & Background Noise
- Playback activity and preferences

Safety & Moderation
- Abuse detection events
- Content filter triggers
- Guardrail violations

Privacy & Compliance
- Consent records (analytics, marketing, functional)
- Data export and deletion requests
- Full audit trail

Platform Metrics (Admin Dashboard)
- User signups and growth
- Task completion rate
- Average focus duration
- AI conversation volume (24h / 7d / all-time)
- 7-day retention
- Most common activities
- Mood distribution
- Assessment completion rates

Data Retention
- Events kept for 30 days
- Conversations kept for 90 days
- Automatic daily cleanup
